Roasted Potatoe

Roasted red potatoes at their best - plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F and adjust the oven rack to the lowest position.
  3. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Arrange, cut-side down, on a rimmed cookie sheet or jellyroll pan.
  4. Roast potatoes in the preheated oven until tender and golden brown, 20 to 30 minutes. Transfer to a serving dish.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
